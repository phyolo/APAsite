Asian Photography Association

The Asian Photography Association (APA) is a non-profit community based in Seattle, founded by passionate Asian photographers in 2025. We are dedicated to fostering cultural exchange, sharing photography skills, and building meaningful connections across communities and art institutions. Through the art of photography, we capture life’s light, colors, and human warmth — helping everyone grow together and share more beautiful stories.

About Us

Founded in 2025 and headquartered in Seattle, USA, the Asian Photography Association (APA) is a nonprofit community organization established by a group of passionate Asian photographers who share a love for both photography and public service.
We are committed to fostering meaningful cultural exchange within our community.Through regular public workshops, open lectures, and free technical training, APA empowers photography enthusiasts while building lasting connections between Asian photographers, media, art institutions, and cultural organizations.

华人摄影协会（Asian Photography Association, APA）成立于2025年，总部位于美国西雅图，由一群热爱摄影与公益的华裔摄影师发起，是一个非盈利社群组织。我们致力于为社区文化交流搭建桥梁，定期举办公益讲座，免费为摄影爱好者提供技术培训与指导，同时积极推动亚裔摄影师与媒体、艺术机构建立联系与合作。我们努力打造一个集摄影作品展示、技术经验交流、资源互助于一体的开放平台，构建一个充满活力、包容与支持的摄影社群。 我们希望通过摄影这一独特的艺术语言，去捕捉生活中的光影与色彩，记录自然与人文的温度，连接多元文化与彼此的心灵，助力大家共同成长，让更多美好被看见，更多故事被分享。
